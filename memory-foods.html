3 Foods That Boost Memory (Backed by Science)

Ever walk into a room and forget why you entered? Brain fog happens to the best of us. Here are the three best foods to fix it.

1. Fatty Fish (The "Gray Matter" Builder)

Why it works: Your brain is 60% fat. Fish like salmon are loaded with omega-3s, which are essential for learning and memory.

2. Blueberries (The "Anti-Aging" Berry)

Why it works: Blueberries provide antioxidants that reduce inflammation and improve communication between brain cells.

3. Pumpkin Seeds (The "Magnesium" Bomb)

Why it works: They are an excellent source of Magnesium, Iron, and Zinc. Low magnesium is linked to brain fog and migraines.
