Digital Minimalism: Unclutter Your Virtual Space

A cluttered desktop leads to a cluttered mind. If you have 50 icons on your desktop and 2,000 unread emails, your brain is constantly running background processes to process that visual noise.

The "One Folder" Policy

Move every file on your desktop into a single folder named "Archive." Suddenly, your screen is clear. If you need something, search for it.

Notification Audit: Go to your phone settings. Turn off ALL notifications except for Phone Calls and Text Messages. Does Instagram really need to interrupt your dinner? No.
