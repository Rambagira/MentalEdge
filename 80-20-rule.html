The 80/20 Rule: Work Less, Achieve More

The Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule) states that 80% of your results come from 20% of your efforts. This universal law applies to business, health, and studying.

Identify the Vital Few

Most of what we do is "busy work" that yields very little result. To apply this principle, you must be ruthless with your time.

  • In Learning: 20% of the vocabulary allows you to understand 80% of the language. Focus on that first.
  • In Business: 80% of sales usually come from 20% of clients.
The Audit: Look at your to-do list for today. If you have 10 items, only 2 of them actually matter. Find them, do them, and ignore the rest.
