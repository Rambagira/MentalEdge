The Growth Mindset: Talent is a Myth

Do you believe intelligence is fixed at birth, or developed through effort? Your answer to this question determines your success.

Dr. Carol Dweck's research identifies two mindsets:

  • Fixed Mindset: "I'm not good at math." (Avoids challenges).
  • Growth Mindset: "I'm not good at math yet." (Embraces challenges).

The Power of "Yet"

When you fail, do not say "I failed." Say "I learned one way that doesn't work." This simple linguistic shift keeps your dopamine levels stable, allowing you to try again without losing motivation.
