Monk Mode: The Nuclear Option for Productivity

Monk Mode is a period of immersion. It is a temporary commitment (usually 21 to 90 days) where you cut out 90% of life's distractions to focus solely on one goal.

The 3 Non-Negotiables

  1. No Alcohol or Substances: Your brain must be running at 100% clarity.
  2. 30 Minutes of Exercise: To keep your energy high and manage cortisol.
  3. Zero Entertainment: No Netflix, no video games, no scrolling. Dopamine is reserved for work only.
Warning: Monk Mode is socially isolating. Tell your friends and family beforehand: "I am going into a deep work phase for 30 days. I will see you on the other side." They will respect your drive.
